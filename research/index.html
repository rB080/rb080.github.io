Research Experience and Interests

I work mostly on Computer Vision problems using Deep learning in a broad sense. The following are the major areas I have worked on:

  • Image Segmentation (Supervised and Semi-supervised)
  • Image Translation (Rib Suppression from Chest X-Rays)
  • Facial Symmetry Detection from 2D human face images
  • Hand Gesture Recognition from hand gesture video snippets
  • Model Calibration under corrupted and perturbed image data
  • Image Translation (Rib Suppression from Chest X-Rays)
  • Weakly Supervised Image Segmentation using Vision Language Models (CLIP)
